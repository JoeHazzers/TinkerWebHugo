Welcome to TinkerSoc
The University of Kent's Maker Society

TinkerSoc is the society where we build, hack and (sometimes) blow things up. TinkerSoc is not just about electronics, but we do like our fair share of LEDs. It is a society that embraces all forms of technology, engineering and craft.

What?

TinkerSoc is for people who love to share their creativity with like minded people, and collaborate in designs and projects that are, simply, awesome. We bring together people from differen backgrounds and skills to work together to make things.
We welcome everyone from all disciplines, whether you are a Law student or an engineer we can guarantee you will learn something new and have fun at our events!
